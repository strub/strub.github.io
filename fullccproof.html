Full CC Proof

The formal Coq development can be downloaded from the following URL: fullcc.tar.bz2

It requires Coq 8.6 & ssreflect 1.6.

The main theorem (5.11) can be found at the end of the file fullcc.v under the name commute. Intermediate lemmas (5.3, 5.5 & 5.7) can be found under the names L_5_3, L_5_5 & L_5_7

It is also possible to browse the development online.
